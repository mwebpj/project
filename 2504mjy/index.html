ABOUT ME

With a background in game planning, I go beyond simple screens to design the underlying context and emotion,
always considering a natural and comfortable user flow.
I pursue minimal design that conveys emotion by removing unnecessary elements and focusing on clarity, readability, and accessibility.
To me, design is clear communication — I aim to deliver a simple yet intuitive experience that feels both comfortable and understandable.
